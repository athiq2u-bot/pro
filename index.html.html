A Digital Companion for Every Farmer

S-Pro addresses the critical need for personalized, timely agricultural advice by providing an AI-powered assistant in Malayalam. It bridges the knowledge gap, turning data into actionable insights right on the farmer's mobile device.

The Ecosystem: Customer Segments

Our model serves a diverse ecosystem, focusing on government procurement to deliver a free, high-impact service directly to farmers, while engaging secondary stakeholders through data.

Primary Customer: Government

Departments of Agriculture seeking to scale extension services, improve scheme delivery, and gather real-time field data efficiently.

End-User Beneficiary: Farmers

Smallholder farmers in Kerala, often with low digital literacy, who need localized, voice-enabled advice to improve productivity.

Secondary Stakeholders

Agri-Input companies and financial institutions benefiting from anonymized data on crop cycles and input usage.

Core Value Exchange

S-Pro delivers distinct, synergistic value to both the government paying for the service and the farmers using it daily. This dual-focus ensures alignment, adoption, and long-term impact.

Engine of Sustainability: Revenue Model

A B2G model ensures the service remains free for farmers. Revenue is generated through recurring government contracts, creating a predictable and scalable income stream tied to impact.

Cost Structure

Our primary investments are in specialized talent and scalable technology. Costs are managed to maximize the margin from recurring licensing fees, ensuring financial health.
